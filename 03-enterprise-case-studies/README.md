## How I work as a PM

A few honest things about how I operate, because they show up consistently across all of these products:

**I diagnose before I prescribe.** Every product on this list started with me spending real time in the data, talking to operators, and understanding the actual failure mode — not just the symptom that leadership was seeing. The churn model at Bell, the fraud platform at TELUS, the CI Dashboard — all of them required reframing the problem before the solution was even discussable.

**I treat stakeholder management as product work.** Two of these products (CI Dashboard, Store Scheduling) nearly died because a key stakeholder refused to sign off. In both cases, I stayed in the room, ran the methodology walkthrough myself, and found a way to turn the objection into a roadmap expansion. That is not an accident — it is how I approach every launch.

**I own confidentiality.** Exact internal model names, team configurations, and specific client data are not reproduced here. Every case study uses directional language where precision would cross into confidential territory. If you want the full detail, let us talk.
