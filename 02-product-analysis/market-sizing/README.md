# Market Sizing

I looked at the e-commerce retention market to understand if this opportunity was worth investing in.

The data showed a $2.5B addressable market. If we execute well, we could realistically reach $37.5M in revenue by Year 5.

That's the number that justified investing engineering resources into fixing churn.
