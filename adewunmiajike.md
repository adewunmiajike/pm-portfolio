## Personal project — SPARK AI Program

**Grant-funded · Built end to end with AI tools · Launched 2026**

SPARK is an AI literacy program I scoped, designed, and shipped independently — from grant application through to a fully deployed website, curriculum, and session materials. It is the most complete example of what I do when I own a product with no engineering team, no design team, and no PM above me.

What this involved as a product problem:

- Defined two distinct user tracks (ages 14–17 and 18–24) with different learning outcomes, session structures, and delivery formats — the equivalent of shipping two product variants simultaneously
- Secured the Fora Global Impact Fund grant by writing a business case for the program's impact and feasibility — the same muscle as an internal funding pitch to an executive sponsor
- Built the full delivery infrastructure using AI: curriculum design, slide decks, handouts, and a multi-page website deployed on Netlify — scoped, designed, and launched in days
- Applied responsible AI principles throughout the curriculum design, not as an afterthought

The SPARK case study is documented in full at [`spark-ai-curriculum`](https://github.com/adewunmiajike/spark-ai-curriculum) *(coming soon)*.
